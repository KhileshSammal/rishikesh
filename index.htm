4-Day Trip: Delhi → Haridwar → Rishikesh → Mussoorie → Dehradun → Delhi (WeekEnd Escape)

Explore the spiritual and scenic beauty of Uttarakhand

Day 1: Delhi to Haridwar

Your spiritual adventure begins at 12:00 AM midnight from Delhi. Travel overnight by car, bus, or train, and reach the holy city of Haridwar by 6:00 AM.

Upon arrival, head straight to Har Ki Pauri, one of the most sacred ghats in India. Take a holy dip (snaan) in the Ganges, believed to wash away sins and purify the soul.

After the rejuvenating morning ritual, walk to the Udan Khatola Ropeway and ascend to the divine Mansa Devi Temple. The ride gives you a panoramic view of the city and river. Seek blessings and enjoy the peaceful aura atop the hill.

Return to the base by 10:00 AM, and take a short stroll or rickshaw ride to the local markets. Have a fulfilling North Indian breakfast—aloo puri, kachori sabzi, jalebi, or lassi at a popular Haridwar dhaba.

Watch: Haridwar Ropeway & Ghat Experience

After breakfast, explore the colorful local bazaars for souvenirs and experience the calm rhythm of Haridwar life. You can hire a cycle rickshaw for ₹80 to tour the narrow heritage lanes of this ancient city.

Around 12:00 PM, leave for your next destination — the yoga capital of India, Rishikesh, just 45 minutes away.

Day 2: Haridwar to Rishikesh (Stay)

After a peaceful morning in Haridwar, head to the spiritual and adventure-packed town of Rishikesh. Begin with a serene walk along the Ganga Ghats, then explore yoga ashrams, try local organic cafés, and in the afternoon – experience the adrenaline rush of river rafting. End your day with the breathtaking Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat.

Adrenaline Rush – River Rafting on the Ganga

Rishikesh is one of India’s top destinations for white-water rafting. The popular stretch from Shivpuri to Rishikesh offers thrilling Grade II and III rapids, surrounded by scenic Himalayan views. Make sure to wear safety gear and go with certified guides.

Day 3: Rishikesh to Mussoorie (via Dehradun)

After enjoying the peaceful vibes of Rishikesh, head to the Rishikesh Bus Stand by 3:00 PM. Take a bus to Dehradun for just ₹100. The journey to Dehradun takes around 1 hour and offers beautiful views of forested hills and the winding Ganges as you leave the valley behind.

Reach Dehradun by 4:00 PM. Head to Prince Chowk, a convenient spot for travelers. Here, rent a scooty for ₹700 (for two days), pickup time scheduled for the next day at 8:00 PM.

Before heading up the hills, stop at the iconic Ghanta Ghar (Clock Tower) and treat yourself to some classic local ice cream—a simple delight in the heart of the city.

Begin your ride to Mussoorie by 5:30 PM. The journey is a breathtaking uphill drive of about 1.5 hours. You'll pass by winding roads, pine forests, and stunning viewpoints of the Doon valley below.

Reach Mussoorie around 7:00 PM and check in at the luxurious Ramada Hotel, located near the Mall Road. Room charges are around ₹4,000 per night, offering comfort and spectacular views of the hills.

After check-in, step out to explore Mall Road — the heart of Mussoorie nightlife. Enjoy hot snacks, street shopping, and scenic viewpoints lit up under the night sky. Have dinner at a cozy café and soak in the cool mountain air.
